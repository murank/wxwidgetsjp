Detailed Description

A wxGraphicsBrush is a native representation of a brush.

The contents are specific and private to the respective renderer. Instances are ref counted and can therefore be assigned as usual. The only way to get a valid instance is via wxGraphicsContext::CreateBrush() or wxGraphicsRenderer::CreateBrush().
