wxFileInputStream

このクラスはファイルからのデータ読み込みを表しています。There are actually two such groups of classes: this one is based on wxFile whereas wxFFileInputStream is based in the wxFFile class.

Note that SeekI() can seek beyond the end of the stream (file) and will thus not return wxInvalidOffset for that.
